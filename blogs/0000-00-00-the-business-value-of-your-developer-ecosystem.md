This article explores how developer ecosystems generate value beyond direct revenue metrics, arguing that developers extend platform reach and solve critical user problems. Moores examines both direct monetization opportunities like the Workday Marketplace and indirect benefits including reduced customer churn, community-led R&D, and developers' influence on enterprise purchasing decisions. The piece makes the case that compound value from community knowledge and network effects is often more significant than direct revenue.
